About Me

This is what I do.

Hi there! My name is Yuqing Xia.

I love data and I believe that nowadays data are more effective at telling truth than words, so I develop myself learning machine learning models, natural language models as well as big data processing tools, like python, Spark.

Among all data scientists, I define myself as a data scientist who are good at story telling and deliver findings in an accessible way to people with non-technical backgrounds.

I believe data speaks, but no researches are perfect that all creates fancy results. Currently, my best model performance using deep learning models can achieve 98% of validation accuracy, but I also have models that only achieve 40% of F-1 scores.

Although project tasks varies, all of my projects you browse here runs from Exploratory Data Analysis, Visualization, to Modelling, Validation and finally to Discussion sessions. Thus I am familiar with most modelling pipelines during my past experiences.

And my data scientists' projects cover different fields (including News/ Media, Environment, Mortgage Banking, technical Products and Skincare Products.) which proves that I am capable of quickly catching up domain knowledge needed in different tasks.
