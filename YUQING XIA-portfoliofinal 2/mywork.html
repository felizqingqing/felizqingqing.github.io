Portfolio1

Help Chinese American Elderly to learn English


Design Problem Statement

In Ann Arbor, there are lots of elderly apartments where some Chinese elderly lived in there. Most of them came from China, however they have American citizens thanks to their children' efforts. Although elderly apartments offer themdecent living environment and diverse activities. They feel helpless when they need to go out on their own because they barely can speak English or have the confidence to communicate with native speakers. Although local community do offer them English learn classes, the teaching style and textbooks are not suitable for them. For example, elderly learn really slow, instead of pouring tremendous informations to them, repeating same contents frequently can help them learn faster. Also, instead of teaching them English in a systematically way as teaching student, we should consider offer them most pratical dialogues they may need in real life.

Solution One

My solutions are two ways. The first approach is that taking elderly guide tours to supermarket, mall, gym, hospital and public bus to help them learn practical English. Those tours will be guided volunteers. And each week they will have a topic to learn and practice. Then, based on that, offering an application to enable elderly to review what have been learned during the guide tour. This app will filled with key words and dialogues, and users can learn the language spelling, pronunciation, and listening skills by frequently repeating those materials.

Solution Two

Another way is that, each month, the elderly apartment can cooperate with local students to establish a social event. A party offers free snacks, meals or home-made meals to appeal for students to join and the only requirement for elderly is that they can only speak English!
